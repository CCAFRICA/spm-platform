## 1. What the Substrate IS

The Vialuce platform has one substrate. The substrate is a **biological architecture** — not metaphorically, structurally. The platform's pipeline mirrors biological systems at every layer:

- **DNA replication at import** — complete fidelity, every base pair preserved, nothing filtered
- **Cell differentiation at aggregation** — raw cells become specialized, typed forms while retaining genome
- **Genetic code transcription at plan interpretation** — instructions extracted from the master code
- **Gene expression at calculation** — context activates specific genes from the full genome
- **Nervous system integration at lifecycle** — sensory → processing → verification → motor command
- **Immune memory at flywheel learning** — every exposure trains faster future response
- **Consciousness at forensics** — the system's ability to observe and diagnose itself

Across these layers, the substrate operates by three biological principles that conventional software architecture does not commit to:

1. **Permeability** — content flows into the substrate without gates that would predict consumer needs
2. **Adaptation** — interpretations evolve through use; the substrate gets more capable with every exposure
3. **Perpetuating inertia of intelligence** — once intelligence is created, it persists and propagates; use creates capability that subsequent use compounds

The substrate is what makes the platform *agentic*: structural classifying intelligence (SCI) and foundational AI agents operate over substrate state coherently because the substrate's properties make their operation possible. Conventional software architecture does not provide this; biological systems do; the substrate is built in the biological lineage to commit to what biology already solved.

The substrate is not "an import pipeline." It is not five specialized pipelines. It is **a multi-layer biological architecture in which one carrier preserves complete fidelity, multiple intelligences interpret what was carried, expression surfaces narrow contextually for their purposes, and immune memory accumulates capability through use**. The shape is hub-and-spoke at the architectural level; biological networks at the connectivity level; cell-genome-context at the expression level.

**The substrate's value:**

1. Human users get one observation surface, one disposition surface, one trust surface across all five SCI domains because the substrate is one substrate
2. Machine learners (the three flywheels — Tenant, Foundational, Domain) aggregate signals across SCI agents because the substrate's signal vocabulary is uniform and the canonical signal surface is one
3. New concepts — new fields, new domains, new references, new normalizations — enter the substrate without architectural friction because the carrier is permeable by design
4. Expression surfaces remain extensible because they consume from the carrier contextually, not from narrowed copies maintained at substrate boundaries
5. Intelligence compounds through use rather than degrading; the substrate's adaptation is constitutive, not bolted on

## 2. What the Substrate's Purpose IS

To **carry every piece of content with complete fidelity at the moment of import**, in a form that downstream interpretation, expression, and learning can operate over coherently and contextually.

This is the architectural commitment Decision 92 makes canonical: *"The data knows when it happened. The business decides how to window it. The engine applies the window at calculation time. Import carries truth. Calculation applies context."*

Carry Everything is the architectural premise. Express Contextually is the architectural method. Together they make the substrate adaptive without registry gatekeeping or pre-classification narrowing.

The substrate's intelligence is *interpretive and expressive*, not *ingestive-narrowing*. SCI does not narrow content at ingestion; it interprets content the substrate has already carried. UI does not display narrowed content; it expresses carried content contextually *(domain example, [ICM]: a commission statement renders carried committed_data through the calculation engine at expression time; the carrier did not narrow to "commission-relevant" content at import).* Reports do not extract narrow subsets; they express carried content for outbound contexts *(domain examples, [ICM]: payroll batch outputs from the Reporting expression surface; comp admin disposition workflows operate over carried content, not narrowed copies)*. The mycorrhizal network analogy makes this concrete: *no tree pre-decides which nutrients it needs. The network carries everything. Each tree's current state determines what it absorbs.* Vialuce's substrate likewise — heterogeneous consumers (SCI agents, expression surfaces, tenants, calculation contexts) cannot pre-specify their requirements; the substrate carries everything because consumer needs are emergent.

## 3. The Substrate's Roles

The substrate has **four roles**. Each is grounded in documented biological analog. Each names a category of substrate work; instances within each role may be multiple.

### Role 1 — Carry (DNA Replication)

The carrier ingests content and preserves it completely. Every column, every value, every piece of metadata flows into substrate state without narrowing at carry time. The biological analog is DNA replication: complete fidelity, every base pair preserved, nothing filtered out by the replication apparatus regardless of which cells will later express which genes.

**The carrier's commitments:**

- **Completeness** — nothing is dropped at carry time. If a file has 47 columns, all 47 are carried. If a column has values the system has never seen, those values are carried. The CLT-14B origin: *"The DNA analogy clarified the architectural error instantly: the system was deleting genes during replication."* Genes deleted at replication cannot be expressed at any later layer.
- **Faithfulness with provenance** — carried content retains its origin. Where it came from, when, in what form, with what raw structure. The substrate cannot lose provenance because expression surfaces depend on it for trust and traceability.
- **Structural pre-classification** (Korean Test compliant) — the carrier may surface structural signals about content (column appears numeric, has 12 distinct values, matches a date pattern) but these are *signals about carried content*, not replacements for it. Structural heuristics use value distributions, data types, and statistical properties — never field-name string matching in any natural language.
- **Sequence independence** — the carrier accepts any file in any order at any time. Carrying does not require prior carrying to have happened. This is a substrate commitment surfaced by DIAG-012: operations performed at import time that require other imports to have happened first violate substrate integrity.
- **Calculation-time binding** (Decision 92) — *import carries truth; calculation applies context*. The carrier extracts source_date and stores raw row content; period binding, entity binding, and normalization binding happen at calculation time when the engine knows the active plan and active period. The carrier does not predict business logic; the engine applies it.

### Role 2 — Interpret (Multiple Specialized Intelligences)

Interpretive agents — both the foundational AI surface and specialized SCI agents — operate over carried content to produce structural classifications. Interpretations are *layered onto* carried content, not in place of it. The biological analog is the multi-system interpretation that occurs in living organisms: nervous systems classify sensory input, immune systems classify foreign entities, gene regulatory networks classify cellular state — each interpreting raw substrate content for its specialized purpose without consuming or destroying that content.

**Vialuce's interpretive layer carries multiple intelligences:**

**Two-tier agent architecture** (per Vialuce Architecture Reference C-01):
- **Foundational Agents** — domain-agnostic. Process structure (data topology, entity resolution, period detection, calculation primitives, reconciliation, insight, resolution). Korean Test holds: zero domain vocabulary in foundational code.
- **Domain Agents** — specialist. Process meaning *(domain examples [ICM]: ICM Domain Agent specializes for compensation calculation rules and component types; future Financial Domain Agent specializes for revenue and expense tracking)*. Communicate with foundational agents through the Intent Action Protocol.

**Five SCI agents** (Plan / Entity / Target / Transaction / Reference per Decision 92):
- **Plan SCI** — recognizes plan rules and structures
- **Entity SCI** — recognizes roster/people/organizational identity data
- **Target SCI** — recognizes goal/quota data
- **Transaction SCI** — recognizes performance/transaction event data
- **Reference SCI** (added Decision 92) — recognizes lookup tables, catalogs, normalization data, alias registries

**Three intelligences at runtime — Decision 64 v2 Dual Intelligence + Decision 92 Reference Intelligence extension** *(Amendment 1, integrated)*:

- **Data Intelligence** *(Decision 64 v2)* — autonomous capability that produces semantic field inventory, measurable dimensions, performance structure potential from carried data alone
- **Plan Intelligence** *(Decision 64 v2)* — autonomous capability that produces requirements manifest, calculation type mapping, interpretation quality from carried plans alone
- **Reference Intelligence** *(Decision 92, additive pillar)* — autonomous capability that produces canonical item registries, alias resolution, normalization authority from carried reference data alone

**Decision 64 v2's Dual Intelligence remains locked and valid.** Reference Intelligence is an additive pillar per Decision 92, operating alongside Data and Plan Intelligence without modifying the dual structure. The substrate carries *three* autonomous interpretive intelligences at runtime; the architectural lock on Dual Intelligence (Decision 64 v2) is preserved; Reference Intelligence is the Decision-92-added pillar. *(Architect-channel note: if a future architect prefers to formally lock triple intelligence as the canonical interpretive structure rather than as an additive composition of Decision 64 v2 + Decision 92, a Decision 64 v3 supersession workstream is the disposition path. That workstream is not blocking DS-021 v1.0 lock and is queued as forward-looking architectural work; see "Forward-looking architectural items" near document close.)*

These intelligences operate independently and converge when present together. **Convergence is observation, not computation** (TMR Addendum 10 Decision 69): the convergence service does not add a computation layer; it observes what the intelligences have already produced and surfaces matches, gaps, and opportunities. Convergence does not depend on AI confidence scores (Decision 70 — graceful degradation when LLM tier underperforms).

**The interpretation role's commitments:**

- **Domain specialization without domain leakage** — each SCI agent specializes for its domain through structural patterns, not through hardcoded vocabulary. Korean Test universal.
- **Registry-derived processing vocabulary** — when interpretations dispatch on or produce primitive identifiers at processing boundaries, those identifiers derive from the canonical registry. The registry is canonical *for what processing operates on*; not for what carrying admits.
- **Structural shape compliance** — when interpretations produce primitive emissions, the emissions conform to registered primitive shapes per the canonical validation surface.
- **Three-level signal capture** (Decision 64 v2): every interpretation produces signals at three levels — Classification (what is this?), Comprehension (how does it behave?), Convergence (what connects to what?). All three feed all three flywheel scopes (Tenant, Foundational, Domain). One correction multiplies into multiple intelligence improvements — the Multiplier Principle.

Interpretations feed back into substrate state as classification signals (Decision 64 v2's three-level signal surface). Carried content remains primary; interpretations enrich it.

### Role 3 — Express (Gene Expression at Multiple Surfaces)

Expression surfaces operate over carried content + applicable interpretations to produce results, displays, modifications, and outbound communications. Expression is contextual: each expression surface decides what to express based on its purpose. The biological analog is gene expression: *"Just as DNA carries the complete genetic code and cellular context determines which genes express, the committed data carries everything and the plan determines what calculates."* The cell does not need every gene transcribed at every moment; cellular context activates the genes that are needed when they are needed.

**Expression surfaces include:**

- **Calculation** — runs computation against carried numeric content + applicable primitive interpretations. The Deterministic Calculation Boundary holds: AI calls above the boundary, math below. 100% reconciliation. SOC1/SOC2/GAAP auditable. The Calculation Sovereignty principle (TMR Addendum 1) makes calculation reproducible regardless of import-time logic. **Calculation criteria are immutable during a calculation run** *(Amendment 3, integrated; serves T1-E905 full scope)*: the active plan that governs a calculation run is established before the run begins and cannot change during execution. A calculation run produces results from a fixed criteria set; if criteria change, a new run is required. This is what makes calculations reproducible, audit-rerunnable, and trustworthy as financial assertions.
- **Observation (UI)** — surfaces carried content + interpretations + calculation results to humans for trust, comprehension, and disposition. DS-013 (Platform Experience Architecture) governs. The Bloodwork Principle: comprehension quality visible at summary level, detail on demand. *(Domain example [ICM]: a commission statement renders calculation results to a sales rep with the underlying transaction data accessible on drilldown; comp admin views render aggregate views across rep populations with disposition controls.)*
- **User Modification (UI)** — accepts user dispositions, corrections, overrides, supplements. User modifications feed back into carrier state and into the canonical signal surface. The Three-Tier Resolution Chain holds: AI proposes → deterministic validation → human authority.
- **Reporting** — produces outbound expression to systems-of-record *(domain examples [ICM]: payroll batches to ADP / Workday / QuickBooks; CFDI invoices to Mexican tax authority; commission liability reports to accounting)*, regulatory contexts (SOC compliance), and customer-facing surfaces *(domain example [ICM]: dispute communications)*. Structured Dispute Management is a Reporting expression — disputes as trust-building, not support burden.
- **Forensics (Consciousness)** — the system's ability to observe and diagnose itself. CC-UAT Dual-Trace methodology (TMR Addendum 10): architecture trace (16-probe structural health) + calculation trace (single-entity forensic execution path). The Forensics surface is the platform's self-awareness; without it the substrate cannot know what it is doing.

**Expression surfaces' commitments:**

- **Read from the carrier, not from private narrowed copies.** When an expression surface needs content, it reads from carrier state via canonical interfaces; it does not maintain a parallel private copy.
- **Express Contextually** — each surface narrows for its purpose at expression time. Carried content is broad and complete; expressed content is contextual and purposive.
- **Vocabulary canonicality at processing boundaries** — when expression surfaces dispatch on primitives, dispatch operates on registry-canonical vocabulary with structured failure on unrecognized identifiers.
- **No stale state** (TMR Addendum 10 Decision 68) — expression surfaces that produce persistent results (Calculation, Reporting) commit to DELETE-before-INSERT. Database UNIQUE constraints enforce architecturally; convention does not. Stale results corrupt every layer that reads them.
- **Five Layers of Proof** at Forensics — Outcome → Population → Component → Metric → Data. Every number traceable to source. Prove, Don't Describe.
- **Calculation criteria immutability during a run** *(Amendment 3)* — once a calculation run begins, the active plan cannot change. T1-E905's full scope: *"Criteria are defined before work begins and cannot be changed during execution."*

### Role 4 — Learn (Immune Memory at Multiple Scopes)

Every interpretation and every expression produces signals. Signals feed flywheels and the IGF substrate. Learning refines future interpretations, surfaces architectural drift, and produces governance-class findings. The biological analog is the immune system's adaptive memory: each exposure produces antibodies; antibodies persist; subsequent exposures benefit from accumulated memory; the system gets faster and more confident with use without any external retraining.

**The Adaptive Immunity model** (DS-016/017): innate immunity (fast, non-specific structural heuristics — milliseconds, no LLM) → adaptive immunity (specific LLM classification with fingerprint storage — slow first time, memory thereafter) → antibody recognition (Tier 1 fingerprint match — instant, zero cost) → cross-reactivity (Tier 2 structural analogy — fast classification from cross-tenant patterns) → affinity maturation (confidence progression with each import, 90% → 95% → 97% → 99%). *"The platform literally develops immunity to known file structures. It gets faster and more confident the more it is used."*

**Three flywheel scopes** (Decision 64 v2 + Vialuce Architecture Reference):
- **Flywheel 1 (Tenant Personalization)** — every tenant's interactions train the system for that specific tenant. Privacy: isolated per tenant, never crosses tenant boundaries, SOC2-clean.
- **Flywheel 2 (Foundational Structural Intelligence)** — structural patterns across all tenants and domains accumulated anonymously. Privacy: aggregated, anonymized, no tenant identifiers, no entity data.
- **Flywheel 3 (Domain Vertical Expertise)** — industry patterns within each vertical accumulated. Privacy: aggregated per vertical, anonymized.

**Three signal levels** (Decision 64 v2):
- **Level 1 — Classification Signal** — *can the AI identify what things are?*
- **Level 2 — Comprehension Signal** — *can the AI understand how things behave?*
- **Level 3 — Convergence Signal** — *can the AI connect the right data to the right requirement?*

**The learning role's commitments:**

- **Single canonical signal surface** — Decision 64 v2's classification_signals. No private signal channels (the plan_agent_seeds anti-pattern is formally excluded — agent-to-agent messaging through private JSONB keys violates the canonical signal commitment).
- **Signal vocabulary uniformity** — signals across SCI agents and expression surfaces share the substrate's canonical vocabulary so flywheel aggregation is coherent.
- **Read-path coherence** — signals are not just written; they are read. Convergence and learning loops actually consume the canonical signal surface to refine future interpretation. The convergence read-path architectural gap (signals currently written but not read for cross-run learning, surfaced by AUD-002 v2) is a substrate violation that must close. Writing signals is not learning; reading them is. *(IRA Phase 3 disposition: G11 is derivable from existing substrate — T1-E906 + T1-E924 together require read-path coherence — and does not require a new locked decision. The specific enforcement mechanism for G11 may warrant a Decision-class specification at Phase 4; that is implementation territory, not v1.0 lock-blocking.)*
- **Mycorrhizal cross-flow** — *"a normalization correction in one tenant improves accuracy for every future tenant"*. Learning at one node propagates through the network. The intelligence network is what makes the platform's compound moat real.
- **Multiplier Principle** — one user correction at Level 2 improves Data Intelligence for that tenant (Tenant scope), gets anonymized into structural patterns (Foundational scope), feeds vertical expertise (Domain scope), AND makes future Convergence matches more accurate. *One correction, four improvements.*
- **IGF substrate integration** — learning that produces governance-class findings flows into the IGF substrate per the four-agent model (IRA / IVA / IMA / ICA) and the eight-tier substrate. The IGF governs the substrate; learning at the substrate informs IGF substrate updates through ICA.

Learning loops feed back into Role 2 (Interpret), refining future SCI emissions and foundational AI behavior. The flywheel turns. The substrate's perpetuating inertia of intelligence is mechanical at this layer.

## 4. The Synaptic State (Communication Substrate Within the Substrate)

The substrate has a sub-architecture that warrants its own named role: **the Synaptic State** is the agent communication substrate within the substrate. It is not the carrier (DNA layer); it is not the expression surfaces; it is the chemical environment through which agents communicate during execution.

**The Synaptic State Specification (v1) commits to:**

- **Biological-not-mechanical communication** — *"Neurons don't send HTTP requests to each other. They share a chemical environment. A neurotransmitter released by one neuron is immediately available to every neuron in the synapse."* The Synaptic State is that shared chemical environment for Vialuce's agents. Replaces traditional agent message-passing (request → response → action with latency per round trip and no learning from communication itself).
- **Observation IS Action** — eliminate the distinction between observing a signal and acting on it. The synapse's presence in the shared surface IS the action — it modifies the execution context for all subsequent operations.
- **Nanobatch learning** — every individual operation can update the shared state, and subsequent operations read the updated state. Learning propagates within a single calculation run.
- **Cost decreases with usage** — a new tenant generates maximum synaptic activity (everything uncertain, everything traced); an established tenant generates minimal synaptic activity (most operations execute silently). Operational cost per calculation is a *decreasing function* of cumulative usage. Inverse of traditional platforms.
- **Synaptic Density execution modes** — full_trace (confidence < 0.70), light_trace (0.70–0.95), silent (≥ 0.95). The substrate adapts its computational intensity based on accumulated confidence per pattern.

The Synaptic State is foundational agent infrastructure. Korean Test universal: synapse types are structural (confidence, anomaly, correction, pattern, boundary_behavior, data_quality, resolution_hint, performance), never domain-specific.

The Synaptic State's relationship to the carrier: the carrier holds substrate *content state* (what the system carries forward as fact); the Synaptic State holds substrate *execution state* (what agents are communicating during a run). Both are substrate. They are distinct in role but coordinated in architecture: the Synaptic State's persistent consolidation produces signals that feed the canonical signal surface (Decision 64 v2), and the canonical signal surface feeds back into pre-run synaptic density.

*(IRA Phase 3 disposition Q1: the Synaptic State sub-architecture framing is correct; Synaptic State is infrastructure that enables the four roles, not a fifth role. It does not carry, interpret, express, or learn — it provides the communication medium through which agents performing those roles coordinate.)*

## 5. Substrate Properties

The substrate exhibits eleven properties. These are *what the substrate IS*, not *what the substrate COMMITS TO*. The distinction matters: properties are constitutive of the substrate's construction (a substrate without permeability is not this substrate); commitments are enforceable obligations the substrate honors.

**P1 — Permeability.** Content flows into the substrate without gates that would predict consumer needs. New concepts enter without architectural friction. The substrate operates by carrying everything because consumer needs are emergent and heterogeneous.

**P2 — Adaptation.** The substrate changes in response to what it carries. Interpretations evolve. SCI agents refine their classifications. The system's ability to make sense of content grows over time. Adaptation is constitutive — not an add-on capability.

**P3 — Progressive Optimization.** Better interpretations, better expressions, better operational behavior emerge through continued use. The substrate gets better the more it is used. Conventional systems degrade with use; this substrate accumulates capability with use.

**P4 — Perpetuating Inertia of Intelligence.** Once intelligence is created in the substrate (an interpretation, a classification, a learned pattern), it persists and propagates. The intelligence has momentum that continues unless explicitly counteracted (Synaptic Density's "Nuclear Clear" exists as the explicit counteraction mechanism).

**P5 — Reaction-Prediction Simultaneity.** The substrate operates in two temporal modes at once. It reacts to current content (here is this transaction, classify it, calculate against it, surface it). It predicts future content (this pattern suggests a clawback condition is forming; this trajectory suggests attainment will land at X). Reaction and prediction are not separate subsystems — they are the same substrate operating in two temporal frames.

**P6 — Sequence Independence.** Imports do not require any prior import to have happened first. The substrate accepts any content unit in any order at any time. Operations that create implicit ordering (entity binding requires prior roster import; convergence requires prior plan import) violate sequence independence and are deferred to calculation time per Decision 92.

**P7 — Heterogeneous Consumer Service.** The substrate serves heterogeneous consumers (multiple SCI agents, multiple expression surfaces, multiple tenants, multiple temporal contexts) without prediction of consumer needs. The mycorrhizal architecture: the network carries all nutrients; each consumer's current state determines absorption.

**P8 — Mycorrhizal Cross-Flow.** Intelligence flows through the substrate across tenant boundaries (anonymized per privacy firewall), across temporal periods, across domains. A correction in one location propagates as capability for every future use of similar pattern.

**P9 — Trophic Resilience.** Removing one piece of content from the substrate creates cascading downstream failures impossible to predict at the time of removal. The substrate's resilience is a function of its completeness; narrowing at carry violates resilience even when the narrowing seems harmless.

**P10 — Graceful Degradation.** When any tier underperforms (LLM unavailable, AI confidence = 0, network failure, classification ambiguous), the substrate degrades gracefully. The Three-Tier Resolution Chain operates: LLM-Primary → Deterministic Fallback → Human Authority. Decision 70: convergence does not depend on AI confidence; deterministic methods take over automatically. The substrate does not abort on uncertainty; it produces lower-confidence outputs with surfacing.

**P11 — Self-Awareness.** The Forensics surface (Consciousness) gives the substrate the ability to observe and diagnose itself. CC-UAT Dual-Trace methodology (architecture trace + calculation trace) is the substrate's introspective mechanism. The substrate cannot trust its own behavior without being able to inspect it.

## 6. Substrate Commitments

The substrate commits to eleven obligations that protect the properties above and that enforcement mechanisms must operationalize. Properties are constitutive; commitments are enforceable.

**G1 — Carry Everything.** *(Carry role.)* The carrier preserves every piece of ingested content completely. No narrowing at carry time. Provenance retained.

**G2 — Express Contextually.** *(Express role.)* Expression surfaces narrow for context at expression time. They do not maintain private narrowed copies of carried content; they read from the carrier and contextualize.

**G3 — Calculation-Time Binding.** *(Carry + Express roles, per Decision 92.)* Import carries truth; calculation applies context. Period binding, entity binding, normalization binding happen at calculation time, not import time. The carrier extracts source_date and stores raw content; the engine binds dynamically using the active plan and active period.

**G4 — Sequence Independence.** *(Carry role.)* Imports are independent. No import requires another to have happened first. Operations that create ordering dependencies are deferred to calculation time.

**G5 — Registry as Canonical Processing Vocabulary.** *(Interpret + Express roles, processing boundaries only.)* Boundaries that dispatch on, validate, calculate against, or transform on primitive identifiers derive their vocabulary from the canonical registry. Private vocabulary copies at processing boundaries are substrate violations. The carrier itself is unconstrained; the registry governs *processing*, not *carrying*.

**G6 — Structured Failure on Unrecognized at Processing Boundaries.** *(Interpret + Express roles, dispatch boundaries only.)* Every dispatch boundary produces named, observable, structured failure on identifiers not registered. No silent default. No silent fall-through. (Decision 154 applies to processing.)

**G7 — Single Canonical Signal Surface.** *(Learn role.)* Classification signals flow to one canonical surface (Decision 64 v2's classification_signals). No private signal channels. Three signal levels (Classification, Comprehension, Convergence). Signals are aggregable across SCI agents and expression surfaces because the surface is one.

**G8 — Korean Test Universally on Structural Identification.** *(All roles.)* Identifying what content structurally is, what a primitive identifier means, what a value range represents — all uses structural heuristics, never field-name string matching in any natural language. AP-25 / Decision 154's extension to operation/primitive/dispatch-surface identifiers.

**G9 — Deterministic Calculation Boundary with Criteria Immutability.** *(Express role, Calculation surface; serves T1-E905 full scope per Amendment 3.)* All AI calls above the boundary; all math below. 100% reconciliation. SOC1/SOC2/GAAP auditable. Calculation Sovereignty: calculation depends only on committed data + active plan at runtime — never on import-time logic. **Calculation criteria (the active plan) are immutable during a calculation run** — once a run begins, criteria cannot change; if criteria change, a new run is required. This is what makes calculations reproducible, audit-rerunnable, and trustworthy as financial assertions.

**G10 — No Stale State.** *(Express role, persistent expression surfaces.)* DELETE before INSERT. Database UNIQUE constraints enforce architecturally. Stale results corrupt every downstream layer that reads them; the substrate prevents stale state from existing rather than depending on application discipline to delete.

**G11 — Read-Path Coherence at the Signal Surface.** *(Learn role.)* Signals are not just written; they are read. Convergence and learning loops actually consume the canonical signal surface to refine future interpretation. The write-only signal surface architectural gap (AUD-002 v2) is a substrate violation that must close. *(G11 is derivable from existing substrate — T1-E906 + T1-E924 together require read-path coherence — and does not require a new locked decision; the specific enforcement mechanism may warrant a Decision-class specification at Phase 4 audit.)*

### Property–Commitment Mapping (Many-to-Many) *(Amendment 2, integrated)*

The eleven commitments protect the eleven properties through a *many-to-many* mapping. Earlier 1b drafting forced a 1-to-1 mapping (G1↔P1, G2↔P7, etc.) which introduced architectural distortion: G8 was paired with P10 (Graceful Degradation) when Korean Test in fact protects P7 (Heterogeneous Consumer Service); G9 was paired with P11 (Self-Awareness) when Calculation Sovereignty in fact protects P5 (Reaction-Prediction Simultaneity); G5/G6 were paired loosely when in fact registry canonicality and structured failure protect P11 (Self-Awareness — observability requires canonical vocabulary).

The corrected mapping reflects actual protection relationships:

| Commitment | Primary property protected | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|

## 7. Convergence as Observation

A separate section because TMR Addendum 10 makes Convergence's role architecturally distinctive: **Convergence is observation, not computation.**

The convergence service does not add a computation layer. It does not classify data (Data Intelligence does that). It does not interpret plans (Plan Intelligence does that). It does not compute results (the engine does that). It does not depend on AI field classification confidence scores (Decision 70).

What Convergence does: observe what the platform already produces and surface what is already true. It performs four operations, all at runtime, all from data:

1. Extract plan requirements from component structure
2. Inventory data capabilities by sampling committed_data rows
3. Match supply to demand via semantic type overlap and keyword scoring
4. Detect shared-base patterns and generate derivation rules

Convergence is *substrate observation*, not substrate computation. This distinction matters architecturally: the substrate has interpretive intelligences (Data, Plan, Reference) that *produce* understanding; expression surfaces (Calculation, Observation, Reporting) that *produce* outputs; learning loops (flywheels) that *produce* refinement. Convergence is the surface that *observes* what these productions have made true and surfaces matches, gaps, and opportunities — without adding new production work.

This is biologically analogous to the proprioceptive system: the body's awareness of where its limbs are and how they relate to each other. Proprioception does not move the limbs; it observes. The information surfaces consumers (motor cortex, balance systems) that act on the observation. Convergence in Vialuce is proprioception at the substrate level.

*(IRA Phase 3 disposition Q4: the four-role decomposition is substrate-coherent as-is. Convergence is a cross-cutting observation function over the four-role products. Elevating it to a fifth role would create a category error — Convergence does not produce substrate state, interpretations, outputs, or refinement; it observes. The current section-level placement is the correct architectural disposition.)*

## 8. Enforcement Model

Enforcement matches each commitment to mechanism(s) appropriate for the role and the boundary type. Layered enforcement remains, but the layers are *role-appropriate* rather than uniformly applied.

**Carrying-side enforcement.** Completeness and faithfulness. Mechanisms: schema-on-import that admits any column structure; provenance metadata required on every carried record; integrity audits comparing carried state to source files; sequence-independence verification (each import succeeds without other imports having happened). Protects G1, G3, G4.

**Processing-side enforcement.** Registry-canonicality and structured failure. Mechanisms (selection is Decision-class, not yet locked):

- *Type-system enforcement* — primitive identifiers constrained to canonical registry type unions (compile-time)
- *Runtime substrate guard* — wrapping every dispatch site, structured failure by construction (runtime)
- *Build-time gate* — registry-grep CI / lint rule flagging private-copy patterns (PR-time)
- *Process discipline* — Standing Rules, code review checklists (review-time)

Layered combination protects G5, G6, G8.

**Expression-side enforcement.** Read-from-carrier, narrow-at-expression, no stale state, criteria immutability. Mechanisms: expression surfaces must consume carrier APIs, not maintain private state copies; UI/Reporting surfaces audit-tested for read-path coherence with carrier; expression-time narrowing is documented; persistent expression surfaces enforce DELETE-before-INSERT through database UNIQUE constraints; calculation runs lock active-plan reference at run start with run-scoped reference invariant. Protects G2, G9, G10.

**Learning-side enforcement.** Signal canonicality and read-path. Mechanisms: classification_signals as the single signal surface (Decision 64 v2 enforced); read-path audit (signals are read, not just written); flywheel aggregation tested against the canonical surface; mycorrhizal cross-flow tested through cross-tenant signal propagation. Protects G7, G11.

**Universal enforcement.** Korean Test discipline (G8), deterministic calculation boundary (G9). Mechanisms: code review gates, structural-heuristic-only enforcement at AI prompt construction, separation of AI and math layers in code organization, automated grep enforcement via architecture trace.

**Substrate self-awareness as enforcement.** The CC-UAT Dual-Trace methodology (TMR Addendum 10) is the substrate's enforcement-by-introspection mechanism. The 16-probe architecture trace tests every layer's structural health; the calculation trace forensically verifies single-entity execution. The substrate enforces commitments by being able to observe whether commitments hold.

Selection of specific enforcement layers within each side is a series of Decision-class architectural decisions (Decision 158-class candidates per earlier framing, but framed against the universal substrate, not the Plan slice).

## 9. EECI Applied to the Substrate

The IGF tetrad evaluates the substrate at every architectural decision.

**Efficiency (E1)** — does the substrate impose the minimum overhead needed to produce its properties and commitments? Carry Everything has efficiency cost (storage, query); Express Contextually recovers efficiency at expression time; the trade-off is correct only if expression-time contextualization is genuinely efficient. Synaptic Density execution modes (silent / light_trace / full_trace) are E1 mechanisms — operational cost decreases with cumulative usage.

**Efficacy (E2)** — does the substrate actually deliver its commitments? G1 through G11 hold or do not at every relevant boundary; efficacy is measured against compliance. AUD-002 v2 evidence (24% Decision 155 compliance, write-only signal surface, plan_agent_seeds private channel) shows efficacy gaps that current architectural work is closing.

**Comprehensive (E3)** — does the substrate cover every SCI agent, every expression surface, every learning loop? Universality means E3 has high weight. Plan-only coherence is not substrate coherence. The substrate must serve all five SCI agents (Plan / Entity / Target / Transaction / Reference), all expression surfaces (Calculation / Observation / Modification / Reporting / Forensics), all three flywheel scopes.

**Innovate (E4)** — does the substrate produce something the platform does not have access to without it? Permeability + Adaptation + Progressive Optimization + Perpetuating Inertia of Intelligence + Reaction-Prediction Simultaneity — these are platform-defining innovations only insofar as the substrate enables them mechanically. The Three-Scope Flywheel (Tenant / Foundational / Domain) is innovation only if the canonical signal surface and read-path coherence are operative; otherwise it is aspiration.

EECI is the evaluation frame at every substrate decision. Decisions scoring high on E1 alone are suspicious; E2/E3/E4 with acceptable E1 is the pattern of substrate decisions worth locking.

## 10. What the Substrate is NOT

To prevent the substrate concept from sprawling:

- **The substrate is not the SCI agents.** SCI agents are interpreters operating *within* the substrate. Their domain specializations are not substrate.
- **The substrate is not the registry.** The registry is the canonical processing vocabulary surface within the substrate's Interpret and Express roles. The substrate is the architecture that makes the registry's authority operationally meaningful.
- **The substrate is not any single expression surface.** The engine, UI, reporting layer, forensics layer — none constitute the substrate. Each is an expression surface that consumes from the carrier.
- **The substrate is not "the import pipeline."** The carrier is not a pipeline; it is a hub. There is no left-to-right flow; there is carry-and-fan-out plus mycorrhizal cross-flow.
- **The substrate is not the Synaptic State alone.** The Synaptic State is the agent communication substrate within the substrate. The substrate also includes the carrier, interpretive intelligences, expression surfaces, and learning loops.
- **The substrate is not the IGF.** The IGF governs the substrate (and the platform broadly) through its four-agent model and tier structure. The IGF is meta-substrate; substrate is platform-state-and-architecture; IGF observes and disciplines substrate behavior at governance boundaries. *(Per T0-E06 + T1-E911: tier authority preserved; DS-021 G1–G11 do not constrain or contradict any T0 entry.)*
- **The substrate is not engineered architecture with biological decoration.** The biological lineage is the substrate's architectural specification, documented across years. Conventional software-architecture patterns (compiler IR, database query planners, OS syscall ABIs) are useful for mining engineering insights but do not capture the substrate's commitments to permeability, adaptation, progressive optimization, and perpetuating inertia of intelligence.

## 11. Biological Lineage Discipline (Load-Bearing or Pruned)

DS-021 grounds in nine biological systems at different scales: DNA replication (carry), Cell Differentiation (aggregation), Genetic Code Transcription (plan interpretation), Gene Expression (calculation), Nervous System Integration (lifecycle), Immune Memory (flywheel), Mycorrhizal Networks (cross-flow connectivity), Trophic Cascades (resilience), Synaptic Transmission (agent communication), Consciousness (forensics self-awareness).

The discipline that makes this multi-system grounding architecturally rigorous rather than decorative collage:

**Each biological analog must carry a falsifiable architectural commitment.** Per AUD-004 v3's three load-bearing analogies discipline (Genetic code, Synaptic transmission, Immune-system pattern recognition), each analog states what it predicts about the substrate's behavior and what would falsify the analogy's architectural claim.

- **DNA replication → completeness at carry.** Prediction: every base pair preserved; gene-deletion at replication is structurally impossible. Falsifier: AI-as-filter pattern at import (the CLT-14B failure mode). The analogy is load-bearing because its falsifier is observable and was historically observed.
- **Immune system pattern recognition → adaptive memory through exposure.** Prediction: pattern recognition is structural (not enumerated); novel pathogens with known structural patterns are recognized. Falsifier: enumerated lists at every boundary that must be kept in sync. The analogy is load-bearing because its falsifier is a current-state architectural failure mode (per AUD-002 v2 evidence).
- **Synaptic transmission → dual-channel signaling discipline.** Prediction: signal channel + neuromodulation channel coexist with read-before-derive obligation. Falsifier: per-service boilerplate read-before-derive (option, not structural property). The analogy is load-bearing because its falsifier is a current-state implementation gap.
- **Mycorrhizal networks → heterogeneous consumer service.** Prediction: network carries everything; consumers absorb based on current state, not pre-specified needs. Falsifier: prediction of consumer needs at carry time. Load-bearing.
- **Trophic cascades → trophic resilience.** Prediction: removing one piece of carried content creates unpredictable cascading downstream failures. Falsifier: silent narrowing at intermediate pipeline stages without cascading failure. Load-bearing.
- **Gene expression → contextual narrowing at calculation.** Prediction: cellular context activates specific genes from full genome. Falsifier: pre-narrowed copies maintained at substrate boundaries. Load-bearing.
- **Cell differentiation → aggregation with retained genome.** Prediction: raw cells become specialized while retaining full genome. Falsifier: aggregation that drops fields not currently classified. Load-bearing.
- **Nervous system integration → lifecycle coordination.** Prediction: sensory → processing → verification → motor command flow with cross-layer signaling. Falsifier: lifecycle layers operating without cross-layer signal availability. Load-bearing.
- **Consciousness → substrate self-awareness.** Prediction: substrate can observe and diagnose itself. Falsifier: substrate cannot inspect its own behavior. Load-bearing (CC-UAT Dual-Trace exists; falsifier is currently not observed).

**The pruning rule:** if a biological analog cannot state a falsifiable prediction about substrate behavior, it does not belong in DS-021's architectural specification. Decorative analogies are excluded by construction. The nine analogs above each pass the load-bearing test.

This discipline addresses the META Principle Inventory's flag that "domain analogies became architecture without being captured as the analogies they originated from." DS-021 captures each analog *as the analog it originated from*, names *the architectural commitment it carries*, and names *the falsifier that would invalidate it*. The biological lineage is architectural specification by this discipline, not decoration.

This is not a hedge. It is a discipline that prevents over-weighting substrate-bounded reviews on substrate-extending proposals. Substrate-bounded review is reliable within the substrate's reach; its authority extends only as far as the substrate's current population reaches.
